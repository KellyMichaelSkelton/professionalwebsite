Greetings Vistor! Kelly prepared quite the bio for you.

Who is Kelly?


Kelly is a curious, energetic and athletic young professional. He loves to travel and try new experiences. On weekdays, you can find him pursuing new education or helping businesses communicate better with their customers and community. On weekends, you can find him with friends at a local coffee shop, hiking mountains or watching sports.

Origin

Kelly was born at Emerson Hospital in Concord, MA. He spent his childhood living in Billerica, a suburb of Lowell, MA. Despite having traveled all throughout the United States, Panama, and Brazil, he has chosen to stay residing in his hometown, commuting to Boston and other nearby cities to pursue education and contribute to the workforce. Kelly has aspirations to one day live and work in New York NY, Austin TX, or San Francisco CA.

Education

After attending Billerica public schools through 8th grade, Kelly chose to apply and was accepted to Shawsheen Technical High School. He tour various trades, setlling into the business technology program. Upon graduation, he chose to commute to UMass Lowell, a closeby research institution, once he discovered their impressive return-on-investment and low tuition. Kelly found success, graduating magna cum laude and earning acceptance in the university's accelerated MBA program, which he completed in just one year. Now, Kelly is attending Coding Temple's full-time python course where he hopes to bridge the gap between marketing and technology within companies for his future career.

Work Experience

Although only 22 years of age, Kelly has already accrued many years of work experience in the business field from choosing to work while attending university. Kelly began as a crew member at McDonald's and an Administrative Assistant at SSG Pools, a local construction company, when he was 16 years old. After becoming an Assistant Manager, he left McDonald's to participate in a few internships for his major, marketing. These experiences included working as a Technology Intern at the UML Office of Technological Commercialization and as a Digital Marketing Intern at IDEMIA. He continued his stay at SSG, being promoted to Tracking Analyst prior to beginning college and eventually assuming the role of Director of Marketing toward the completion of his undergraduate program. Toward the end of his MBA program, Kelly took on a new position at FIber Optic Center, a global leader in fiber optic cable assembly distribution, as a Systems Architect - primarily focused on Salesforce administration and data integration.
